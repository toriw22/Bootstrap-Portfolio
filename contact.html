About Me

My name is Tori White and I am 25 years old. I live in Yorba Linda with my parents, 2 dogs, and 22 year old sister. I also have a 29 year old sister who lives in Carlsbad. This October, my fiancee and I are getting married. He and I will be taking a roadtrip to Yellowstone for our honeymoon.
Some hobbies I enjoy are hiking, camping, kayaking, archery, piloting my drone, printing objects with my 3D printer, making leather goods, painting, and knitting. I love listening to music and binge watch TV shows and movies. One of my favorite movies is Pan's Labyrinth.
